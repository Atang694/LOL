Background

Home Sweet Home Construction (PTY) Ltd is is a customer focused control system engineering company specialising in SCADA, RTU, automation, control, telemetry systems. The business was incorporated in 2003 and is 100% Motswana owned and operated. The company is currently headed by the Managing Director, Bonny Tiroyamodimo Wadikonyana who has over 2 decades of professional experience having worked in both private and public sectors The company employs a team of experienced and professional engineers with extensive amounts of experience in the industry. The services that we have provided to our clients so far are of world class standard. As an electrical engineering company, HSH Engineering provides electrical design services to tightly integrate both the electrical infrastructure and control system to ensure the entire electrical system and associated processes function as intended. HSH Engineering provides all facets of control system engineering services from project management and conceptual design right the way through to final site commissioning. In addition to this, HSH Engineering provides a dedicated 24x7 support service.
