Background

Home Sweet Home Construction (PTY) Ltd is a customer-focused control system engineering company specialising in SCADA, RTU, automation, control, and telemetry systems. Incorporated in 2003, it's 100% Motswana owned and operated. The Managing Director, Bonny Tiroyamodimo Wadikonyana, has over two decades of experience across public and private sectors.

The company employs a team of professional engineers with extensive industry experience, offering world-class services that help clients meet legal obligations and protect their engineering projects.

As an electrical engineering company, HSH Engineering integrates electrical infrastructure with control systems to ensure functionality. Services range from project management and conceptual design to final site commissioning, with 24/7 dedicated support.

Mission

Our mission is to provide Botswana and beyond with the best services and products to meet immediate and future engineering needs at affordable rates. We rely on a locally-sourced resource pool, integrated processes, advanced technology, and sustainability that protects future generations.
